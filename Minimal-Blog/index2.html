Goodbye, 😢

Dear diary,
I couldn't stand being bullied at school anymore. The insults, mockery and bullying had become unbearable. So I decided to run away for a few days to meet my boyfriend who lives in the south of Europe. I saved up some money, but not enough to buy a plane ticket, so I just left with my backpack. It was hard to lie to my parents, but I needed to escape. When I arrived, I realized that I couldn't stay at my boyfriend's house forever, and that I had to find a way to get away from my parents. So I decided to look for an internship in the south, at my favorite video game publisher. I contacted the company and they accepted to host me. They gave me a tour of the company for a whole afternoon, it was great! I left them a little message on Google map with my personal gmail account ;) I can't wait to start my internship and learn more about the video game industry. I am determined to prove to my parents that I am capable of making decisions and taking care of myself. I hope this internship will help me gain confidence and discover new opportunities for my future. In the meantime, I will take advantage of these few days to relax and recharge. I am grateful to have a loving and supportive boyfriend, and I hope that everything will go well for me in the future. See you soon, dear diary.

Published 10 February 2023
